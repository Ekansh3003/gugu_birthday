The Special Song

This song ki lyrics reminds me of you. Ill tell you a story. Ek ladka tha jo kuch nhi bolta tha chup chap rehta tha. Ek ladki thi jo chup hi nhi hoti thi ek dam fun loving. Usne uss ladke ki main khushiyan bhar di aur use uski tarah bana diya ek dam life-loving and happy. But khud ek dam kaam-kaam karne lag gyi and ladke jaise ban gyi. And the story continues till date. Tu samajh gyi kaunsa gaana hai. Anyways yeh jo linein hain. Listen to every word. Only then you will understand kaise tune mujhe badal diya
